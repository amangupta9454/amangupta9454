Our Speciality's

Knee Surgery

A Knee Surgeon is a specialized type of orthopedic surgeon who focuses on diagnosing, treating, and performing surgical procedures on the knee joint and its associated structures.
This role requires an in-depth understanding of the musculoskeletal system, especially the knee's ligaments, tendons, cartilage, and bones.

Brain Surgery

A Brain Surgeon, also known as a Neurosurgeon, specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and surgical intervention for conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system.
This role requires extensive education, precision, and expertise in handling highly delicate and complex surgeries.

Plastic Surgery

A Plastic Surgeon specializes in reconstructive, aesthetic (cosmetic), and corrective surgeries involving the skin, face, body, and extremities.
Their expertise lies in restoring form and function after trauma, illness, or congenital defects, as well as enhancing or altering physical appearance for aesthetic purposes
